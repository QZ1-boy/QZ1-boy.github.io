Biography

Yi Xiao is a Ph.D. candidate at Wuhan University, under the supervision of Qiangqiang Yuan (袁强强) and Liangpei Zhang (张良培). He received his bachelor's degree from China University of Geoscience and Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Drawing upon the realms of remote sensing image/video processing and computer vision, including:

  • Remote Sensing Information Processing: Hyperspectral/Multitemporal Remote Sensing, Long-time Series Product
  • Computer Vision: Remote Sensing Image/Video Enhancement/Super-resolution, Low-level Vision
  • Machine Learning: Deep Learning, Combine Model and Data-Driven Approach
